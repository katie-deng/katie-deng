bloom - adobe xd

This here is the final project of ATLS 1100: Design Foundations, the introduction course to the CTD program at UCB. This project allowed us to practice the stages of design:

1. Empathy / POV

2. Ideation

3. Paper Prototype

4. Digital Prototype

5. Final Product

The theme of the project is to develop / ideate an app that an individual would use during their alone time. Collaborating with peers, we developed a self-care app in the form of gardening. We utilized Adobe XD to create a mockup of the app.The final product can be found here. For more information about the whole process, check out my progress blog here.
