reframing portrait - adobe lightroom

Reframing Portrait is the first project of Image, where we were given the opportunity to experiment with various forms of photos: from still-life to moving images. This first project had us illustrate a narrative through visuals and words through Adobe Lightroom. I based my book off of the chorus of La La Lost by NIKI.

Through the photos and lyrics, I was able to showcase the feelings of coming of age with my college friends, as we were all going on different paths after spring semester. To see the cover of the book, click here. To see the contents of the book, click here.
