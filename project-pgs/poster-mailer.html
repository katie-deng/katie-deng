poster mailer - adobe indesign

This poster mail was our second project for Text. Another physical project, we were tasked to make a tabloid sized informational poster mailer, using information from the Colorado Symphony. In addition to using expressive type skills from the first project, this project emphasized type and text layout. Backgrounds were created in Adobe Illustrator and layout was done on Adobe InDesign.

My inspiration for the poster mailer was the "romantic era". There were lots of florals and texture on my moodboard. Although less typographic than the project (and professor) intended, I am quite satisfied with the outcome. For better quality of the project, click here.
